PROJECT MY HOME

About the project

The goal of the project is to create an online service that simplifies the process of selection and documentation of a mortgage. At the moment, documentation of a mortgage loan is a rather complex and time-consuming procedure both in terms of choosing the most favorable financial conditions and proper filling in and filing of the necessary documents. My Home significantly simplifies these tasks, by providing the user with all information about all currently available the financial offers as well as by helping him / her to correctly fill in and send the necessary documents to the Bank. * 1

Why are we Convinced that the project My Home will it be successful?

Because even at the initial stage, the project has a number of undeniable advantages.

For Users:

  • There is no need to examine and compare each financial proposal separately. The service provides an opportunity to analyze conditions of all available offers based on the most important parameters for the user, such as an interest rate, amount of a down payment, amount of monthly payments, etc.;
  • When filling in only one electronic form, the user has an opportunity to send out applications *1 for a mortgage to all banks marked as priority;
  • Guarantee of correctness of filling in and submission of documents sent by the user in an electronic form; *2
  • My Home is an international service, providing information on the possibility of purchasing real estate anywhere in the world, without limiting the user's choice to local offers;
  • Possibility of obtaining a mortgage loan at a reduced interest rate through the implementation of rights to social benefits, subsidies, national incentive, and support programs;
  • Possibility to purchase real estate in installments through the use of programs provided by developers;
  • Obtaining up-to-date information on the possibility of reducing the financial burden, by restructuring the existing mortgage loan;
  • Saving user's time;
  • Possibility to file the application without leaving the comfort zone, possibility to interrupt and return to filling in the application at any convenient time;
  • Reducing stress caused by the need to visit a Bank branch, substantiate one's financial situation as well as elimination of phobias associated with possible refusal to grant a loan;
  • The service provides all the above advantages to its user absolutely free of charge, without requiring anything in return.

Advantages Of Banks:

  • The choice of users, including financial products, is more and more often based on information received from Internet sources. Participation in the project will allow to convey information about the Bank's products and services to a much larger number of potential customers;
  • As a consequence, the increase in the volume of applications, the volume of mortgage loans granted;
  • Reduction of financial and labor costs for personnel in relation to consulting, filling in and verification of documents provided by users;
  • Centralization of the process of verification of submitted applications, documents;
  • Improving the quality of documents provided by borrowers;

These are just a number of advantages that would attract the attention of users. The further goal of the project is the maximum simplification of the procedure for obtaining a mortgage, possibility of its online receipt by filing and signing documents in compliance with provisions of the national legislation on the use of cryptographic methods in transactions, legislation on electronic mortgages.

  • *1 For banks participating in the project;
  • *2 The documents shall be submitted in electronic form in the form of the Bank.
